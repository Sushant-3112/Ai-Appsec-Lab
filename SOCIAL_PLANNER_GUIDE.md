## 📝 How to Use

### Step 1: Access Social Planner
1. Go to Dashboard
2. Click "📅 Social Planner" tab
3. Or visit `/social-planner` directly

### Step 2: Schedule Your First Post

**Method 1: Quick Schedule**
1. Click "Start Planning Today" or "+ New Post"
2. Select platform (Instagram, YouTube, Twitter)
3. Choose media type (Image, Video, Text)
4. Write your content
5. Add hashtags (optional)
6. Pick date and time
7. Click "Schedule Post"

**Method 2: AI-Assisted**
1. Click "Generate Ideas" in AI Content section
2. Review AI suggestions
3. Select a suggestion
4. AI pre-fills caption and hashtags
5. Customize as needed
6. Schedule

### Step 3: Review Scheduled Posts
- View all scheduled posts in calendar view
- Edit or delete posts
- Check post status (Scheduled, Published, Failed)
- Track engagement after publishing

### Step 4: Optimize with AI
- Review "Best Time to Post" suggestion
- Use trending hashtags
- Follow content ideas
- Apply engagement boosters

## 🎯 Best Practices

### Content Planning

**Weekly Planning:**
```
Monday:    Motivational quote
Tuesday:   Tutorial/How-to
Wednesday: Behind-the-scenes
Thursday:  Product/Service highlight
Friday:    Fun/Entertaining content
Saturday:  User-generated content
Sunday:    Weekly recap
```

**Posting Frequency:**
- Instagram: 1-2 posts/day
- Twitter: 3-5 tweets/day
- YouTube: 1-2 videos/week
- TikTok: 1-3 videos/day
- LinkedIn: 1 post/day

### Hashtag Strategy

**Best Practices:**
- Use 3-5 relevant hashtags
- Mix popular and niche hashtags
- Create branded hashtags
- Research trending hashtags
- Avoid banned hashtags

**Example:**
```
Popular:    #TechTips (1M+ posts)
Niche:      #WebDevTips (50K posts)
Branded:    #SmartLinkTips (your brand)
```

### Caption Writing

**Formula:**
1. **Hook** (First line grabs attention)
2. **Value** (Provide useful information)
3. **CTA** (Call-to-action)
4. **Hashtags** (3-5 relevant tags)

**Example:**
```
🚀 Want to 10x your productivity? [Hook]

Here are 5 tools I use daily:
1. SmartLink for link management
2. Notion for note-taking
3. Figma for design
... [Value]

Which one will you try first? 👇 [CTA]

#ProductivityTips #TechTools #WorkSmart [Hashtags]
```
